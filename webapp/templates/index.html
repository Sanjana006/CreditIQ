02

Credit Score is the Strongest Predictor

Borrowers with a CIBIL / FICO score below 670 default at nearly three times the rate of those above 740. A high DTI compounds the risk further — the data confirms that dual stress (low score + high obligations) is the clearest early-warning signal.

04

Underwriting Recommendations

Derived from model output and portfolio analysis — aligned with RBI prudential norms.

✓ Approve
  • CIBIL Score ≥ 720
  • DTI < 35% & FOIR < 50%
  • Zero delinquencies (24 months)
  • Income verified (Source / Full)
  • Revolving utilisation < 50%
  • Model PD < 35%
⚑ Manual Review
  • CIBIL Score 680 – 720
  • DTI 35–45% or FOIR 50–60%
  • 1 delinquency in 24 months
  • Employment < 2 years
  • Revolving utilisation 50–75%
  • Model PD 35–60%
✗ Decline
  • CIBIL Score < 680
  • DTI > 45% or FOIR > 60%
  • ≥ 2 delinquencies in 24 months
  • Public record on file
  • Revolving utilisation > 75%
  • Model PD > 60%
₹ Ind AS 109 — ECL Stages
  • Stage 1 — DPD 0–29 · 12-month ECL
  • Stage 2 — DPD 30–89 · Lifetime ECL
  • Stage 3 — DPD 90+ / NPA · Lifetime ECL
  • LGD: 55% unsecured · 45% MSME
  • ECL = PD × LGD × EAD
